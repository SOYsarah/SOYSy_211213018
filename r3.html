This Simple Green Salad with Homemade Mayo Dressing is a delicious and easy addition to dinner! A perfect salad for fresh summer greens. Ready in 5 minutes. This salad dressing is made with 3 simple ingredients: mayonnaise, milk and sugar. SO simple. But sometimes the most simple things are the best, and this dressing is proof of that!

Simple Mayo Dressing:
>5 tbsp mayonnaise >3 tbsp milk >2 tsp sugar

Step 1: Using a hand blender, whip the mayo, milk and sugar together until creamy. Step 2: Adjust the flavour to your taste. Step 3: Drizzle over your salad of fresh greens. Step 4: Refrigerate when not in use.
