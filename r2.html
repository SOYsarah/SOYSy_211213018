Total Time 15mn
Prep Time 8mn mn
Calories 526


Crispy flour tortillas loaded with chicken, vegetables, and cheese – everything you’d expect in an authentic Mexican style dinner, ready in just 15 minutes. These homemade quesadillas are so EASY to make.

Ingredients of Chicken Quesadilla

2 Servings

>2 flour tortillas (8 inch tortillas)
>1/2 lb chicken breast
>1 small red pepper
>1/4 medium onion
>1 Tbsp taco seasoning
>1 cup shredded cheese
>1 Tbsp butter
>1 Tbsp oil

Step 1: Cut the chicken into small strands. Slice the pepper and onion into thin strands. Step 2: In a skillet, saute the chicken with 2 tsp oil. Season with the taco seasoning and cook until chicken is fully cooked. Remove from the skillet and cover to keep warm. Step 3: Saute the peppers and onions with butter until vegetables reach desired tenderness. Add the chicken back and cook until chicken is heated. Step 4: Add half of the chicken mixture to one side of the tortilla, top with cheese and fold the tortilla. Repeat with the second tortilla. Step 5: In a skillet, cook the quesadillas over medium until the tortillas are crispy and cheese melts.
